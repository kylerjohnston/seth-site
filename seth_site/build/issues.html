The Issues

Socialized Medicine
Obamacare has forced high deductible plans on working people that still leads them to not get care in fear of medical debt. Medicare expansion has been blocked by many Republican governors including our own. We need to make sure every Mainer has access to high quality care, our health is not a place for profit motives.
Full employment with a $15 minimum wage
The state should pursue a policy of full employment, anyone looking for work in Maine should be able to find a position that allows them live and eat. Currently the state is engaged in a race to the bottom with TIFs and various tax breaks to lure in business. When the state negotiations deals with companies looking to come to Maine employment figures should be part of the bargain.
Support for Refugees
Maine should be open to everybody. The state should be coordinating with the federal government to take on refugees fleeing the Syrian civil war. Maine is facing an aging shrinking population and these refugees will offer a much needed boost to our state.
