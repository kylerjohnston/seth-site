Seth Baker for Maine State Senate District 27

I’m excited to share the news that I’m running here in Portland’s State Senate district 27 for the open seat as a Green. I’m looking forward to getting people to think about the issues and what they want for the state and society as whole. We have all sorts of wealth and technology: how do we configure it in a way that will benefit everyday people?
